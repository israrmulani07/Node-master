*Var
-var can be declare and assign
-var can be reassign
-var can be redeclare
-var is mutable
-var can be hoisted.

*let
-let can be declare and assign
-let can be reassign
-let cannot be redeclare
-let is mutable
-let cannot be hoisted.

*const
-const must be declare and assign
-const cannot be reassign
-const cannot be redeclare
-const is immutable
-const cannot be hoisted.
